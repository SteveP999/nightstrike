NightStrike

NightStrike brings late-80s sleaze rock back to life with arena-sized hooks, screaming guitars, leather-jacket swagger, and choruses built for windows-down midnight drives.

The sound is loud, glossy, dangerous, and built for the big stage: high-voltage guitars, gang-vocal hooks, and the kind of attitude that kicks the door open before the first chorus lands.
