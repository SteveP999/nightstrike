About

Parsons Cross

Parsons Cross is a Contemporary Christian worship band rooted in honest faith — the kind that doesn't flinch from doubt, grief, or the long stretches where the answers don't come easy.

Their music starts in the valley and points toward the light. Not because the hard parts get edited out, but because they've found that the hard parts are exactly where grace tends to show up.

Released through Hello Texas Records, their debut album Out of the Darkness walks through five songs of struggle, surrender, and hard-won hope. The follow-up, Into the Light, continues the journey — still honest, now hopeful.
